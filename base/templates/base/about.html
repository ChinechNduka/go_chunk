About Us

Go-Chunk is a non-profit, open-source project launched in 2022 by Zuri Team powered by Ingressive for Good APIs. Having worked with data scientists and analysts all over the world, Go-Chunk aims to offer solutions to the stress of going through so many processes to break down data files and also the loss of files on any device.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer a platform to users that work with enormous data files, such as data analysts, data scientists, developers, and others, where they can split those files into a certain number of sections to make their files easier to work with.

Go-Chunk provides guaranteed security by protecting our users privacy. All actions directly related to the project are covered by the Go-Chunk privacy policy. This is to ensure users can freely perform tasks on the project with safety and assured security. Check out our Terms and Conditions to know our legal propositions.

We also provide our users with the options to download or save their files in their desired formats. Users can always come back to download their files within a certain time frame. Click on our terms and Conditions, for more details.
